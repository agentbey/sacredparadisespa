Established in 2010 by Carolina Ruiz, Sacred Paradise Spa enhances wellness by providing a high-quality spa experience at affordable prices. All services are performed by board-licensed therapists leveraging the world renowned Sacred Paradise techniques (rated #1 by several Celebrities & Athletes). Sacred Paradise understands the importance of maintaining consistency in the quality of the client spa experience.

In addition to providing on-going training and development Sacred Paradise is proud to provide its team members a comfortable living wage – nearly twice the average hourly rate paid at some national massage chains. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, as well as HSA & FSA benefits. As a multi-cultural facility, we speak: English, Spanish, Portuguese and Italian.

Ethics

All services are for therapeutic purposes only. Please do not contact Sacred Paradise Spa if you are seeking any kind of illegal / inappropriate sexual or erotic / sensual services. Sexual advances will not be tolerated at our spa. Any inappropriate behaviors will result in the immediate termination of the services. Clients and staff are not allowed to exchange personal information of any kind with one another. If you have any questions please contact spa directly.

Guests
No children or extra guests are allowed at the spa.

Cell phone
Use of a cellphone is not permitted within the spa in order to keep noise levels to a minimum.

Gratuity
A gratuity is not included unless otherwise stated. In appreciation of outstanding services, gratuities may be given at your discretion at the front desk (typical range: 15-20%).

Cash gratuity, is welcomed and appreciated by all of our wellness practitioners. You can put tips in the therapist envelope(s) located on the front desk.

Cancellation
We require 24 hours notice to cancel, or we will have to charge you the full price of your services. We can not accept same day cancellations. All canceled appointments should be canceled by phone. We do not accept emails for appointment cancellation.

There is no refund on prepaid appointments.

In the event of a no call/no show, the cost of your FULL SESSION will not be refundable.

Scheduling
Massage and services are scheduled by appointment only.

All appointments must be booked on our online portal. Appointments will not be made or scheduled over the phone and will only be accepted via the online portal, no exceptions.

Should you arrive late, your appointment may be shortened to accommodate other guest(s) who have an appointment. Late arrival over 10 minutes may result in cancellation of your appointment.
